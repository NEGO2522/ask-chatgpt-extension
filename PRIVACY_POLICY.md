# Privacy Policy — Ask ChatGPT Chrome Extension

Last updated: May 2026

## Data Collection
This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## How it works
When you select text and click "Ask ChatGPT", the selected text is 
only used to open ChatGPT in a new browser tab. No data is saved 
anywhere by this extension.

## Third Party Services
This extension opens ChatGPT (chatgpt.com). Your use of ChatGPT 
is subject to OpenAI's own Privacy Policy at https://openai.com/privacy

## Permissions Used
- contextMenus: To show the "Ask ChatGPT" option on right-click
- tabs: To open ChatGPT in a new tab
- chatgpt.com access: To auto-fill your selected text into ChatGPT
